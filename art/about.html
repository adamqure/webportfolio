About the Author

I actually have no experience in flower arranging, but I have always enjoyed different arrangements and wondered what they were for.

Flower arranging is not typically seen as an art, but it can be one of the most beautiful art forms in the world. Every girl dreams of meeting that guy who brings her flowers every day and having a beautiful garden for all to see. The nature of flowers inspires awe and wonder in every one who sees them.
